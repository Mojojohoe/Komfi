My Umbrella

Umbrellas?

Online roleplay and BDSM culture often have special phrases and secret codes that participants use. For Example, one phrase is "Paper Tiger," which means a person who portrays themselves as a dom but is actually submissive.

The phrase “My Umbrella” (or these days more commonly the emoji ☂️), comes from Victorian times when women used parasols to communicate their more promiscuous intent without the fear of chastising men, much like fans.

It's used in some roleplaying communities to invert the meaning of certain key words. “Umbrella dom” effectively being an alternative use for the word “paper dom”.

Komfi has taken this a little further and as an additional feature. By clicking the umbrella editor icon on the top toolbar, you can insert a word, or multiple words that will be hidden at the bottom left of the page. When moused over they will display these words prefaced by the umbrella emoji. These words (to those in the know) will mean the opposite.

For example ☂️“Pred” would mean prey. ☂️“Unwilling for men” would mean “Willing for women”. etc.
It's recommended you keep it simple, but you can also insert a closed umbrella 🌂 to signify the word is normal. ☂️“Hate🌂Vore” Hiding words under the secret umbrella is not required.
